SOF ROBOTICS CLUB

Are you interested in joining the new club and participate in a city competition? Come join our club and work with your peers in small teams. You will work in one of the following teams:

  • building the robot
  • programing the robot,
  • troubleshooting and inspecting the robot,
  • club advertising
  • fundraising.

Our competition event qualifiers will take place on January 14 and February 18 at different locations in the city.

More information about the city competition can be found at www.nycfirst.org

We will meet in room 103 on Monday and Wednesday right after school. Check out our new version of the club's website for more details.
