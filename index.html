SOF ROBOTICS CLUB

Are you interested in joining the new club and participate in a city competition? Come join our club and work with your peers in small teams. You can choose one of the following roles:

  • build the robot
  • program the robot,
  • troubleshoot and inspect the robot,
  • club advertising
  • fundraising.

Our competition event qualifiers will take place on January 14 and February 18 at different locations in the city.

More information about the city competition can be found at www.nycfirst.org
